TIPS [With Energy]

  • Level up your Axies in Adventure Mode and advance to high level Ruins. More SLP is gained and makes it easier to reach 100/100 limit when playing in high level Ruins.
  • Try to level up your Axies to suggested levels before challenging higher level Ruins to maximize energy use.

TIPS [No Energy]

  • Get 5 wins in Arena Mode.
  • Play Adventure until max 100/100 SLP is achieved.
  • Complete daily quest and claim your 50 SLP reward.
  • Try to challenge higher Ruin levels to study enemy patterns.
  • You can complete 150 SLP daily even without energy.

Damage Advantage

Buffs

  • Speed Up – Increases Axies Speed by 20%. For a designated time. Can stack.
  • Morale Up – Increases Axies Morale by 20% . For a designated time. Can stack.
  • Attack Up – Increases Axies Attack by 20% . Stays until next attack. Can stack.

Debuffs

  • Stun – Axie skips its next attack or defense. Stays until Axie tries to attack.
  • Aroma – Axie will draw all incoming attacks. For a designated time.
  • Chill – Axie cannot enter last stand. For a designated time.
  • Fear – Axie cannot attack. For a designated time.
  • Fragile – Shield takes 200% damage. For a designated time.
  • Jinx – Axie cannot score critical hits. For a designated time.
  • Lethal – Next hit on this Axie is always a critical strike. Stays until attacked.
  • Poison – Axie loses 3hp each turn taken (by either team). Can stack. Stays until its removed.
  • Sleep – Axie cannot defend next time it is hit. Stays until Axie tries to attack.
  • Stench – Axie is ignored when there are 2 or more targets. For a designated time.
  • Speed Down – Decreases Axies speed by 20%. For a designated time. Can Stack.
  • Morale Down – Decreases Axies morale by 20%. For a designated time. Can Stack.
  • Attack Down – Decreases Axies attack by 20%. Stays until next attack. Can Stack.
